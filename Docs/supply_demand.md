---

## Demand Zone

A **Demand Zone** is an area where strong buying pressure pushed the price higher.



## Supply Zone

A **Supply Zone** is an area where strong selling pressure pushed the price lower.



## Key Rules

✅ Trade with the trend.

✅ Wait for confirmation.

✅ Always use Stop Loss.

✅ Aim for at least 1:1 Risk-to-Reward.

---

### Summary

* Demand Zone = Buy Area
* Supply Zone = Sell Area
* EMA 200 = Trend Filter
* Follow the trend and manage risk.
